Light-winged Smoke, Icarian {{madlib.animal}},

Melting thy pinions in thy {{madlib.direction}} flight,

Lark without song, and messenger of {{madlib.timeOfDay}},

Circling above {{madlib.place}} as thy nest;

Or else, departing dream, and shadowy form

Of {{madlib.time}} vision, gathering up thy skirts;

By night star-veiling, and by day

Darkening the {{madlib.noun}} and blotting out the sun;

Go thou my incense upward from this hearth,

And ask the gods to pardon this {{madlib.color}} flame.
